Mindstorms Experience

In the winter of 2013, I started a private FLL Robotics league to learn about the basics of programming and logic. It was taught by Anant Narayanan, Founder of the McLean Robotics Institute. In sixth Grade, I took part in the Potomac School FLL Robotics league. My team, The Bot Builders, performed very well at the reigonal championship and made it to the state tournament. In seventh grade, I joined the Virginia Tech creative robotics course at VT's northern Virginia campus. This was not a competitive course but it was a ten week program to prepare me for VEX iq next year...
