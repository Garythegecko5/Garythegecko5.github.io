How to download a Stratosfight game

First, click the download button. The zipped folder should appear at the bottom of your browser. Next, drag the file, once it has downloaded, onto your desktop. Right click on the zipped folder and click "Extract All." A window should pop up asking you to select a destination. Simply push the button labled "Extract." Once it is extracted, open the new "Stratosfight" or "Stratosfight 2" folder. Then, open the "exe.win-amd64-3.6" folder. In this folder, run "Game_Alpha." Once you run it, a black screen should appear. Leave it open long enough for the game to load. If it takes longer than 5 minutes, your computer is not supported and the game will not work.

Info and Controlls

Move the player up and down using your mouse, To fire at the enemies, press space. This game only works on windows computers.
