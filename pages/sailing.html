Introduction

I started sailing in 2014. The first boat I learned how to sail in is the Sunfish. It is a light, one or two man single sail boat for small racing rigattas. After I Earned my licence in thees boats I moved onto the Flying Scot. The Flying Scot is a larger, cruising boat meant for pleasure sailing.

Sunfish

I have my smallboat sailor certification on this boat and I have raced it many times with a partner in sunfish rigattas.

Flying Scot

At my camp, I was tested on my knowledge of the nomenclature of the flying scot as well as sailing ability in low and high wind days. This icluded live man overboard procedure and high wind 'suicide dockings.' I passed the test on my third attempt, before anyone else in my class and was awarded the rank "Flying Scot Skipper."
